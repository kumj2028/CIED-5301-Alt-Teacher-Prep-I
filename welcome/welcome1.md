# Welcome to the SHSU Teacher Certification Program

Welcome to the Sam Houston State University teacher certification program! Our program provides an accelerated pathway for individuals who want to become a certified teacher but do not yet hold a valid teacher certification. Through this online platform, you will have access to comprehensive coursework, interactive activities, and practical teaching experiences to prepare you for success in the classroom.

## Certification Process

This Blackboard course organization is designed to guide you through several key steps in the certification process:

- Passing the teacher certification exam(s)
- Completing required observation and field experience hours
- Completing required coursework

## Flexibility and Support

This program is designed to be flexible and accommodating to your schedule, allowing you to balance your professional and personal obligations while pursuing your certification. Our experienced instructors, field supervisors, and support team are committed to helping you achieve your goal of becoming a certified teacher.
