# Getting Started

To get started, complete the following steps:

1. **Work through the Certification Exam Requirements learning module.** In this module, you will learn about the steps required for successfully completing your teacher certification exam requirements. You must pass the applicable certification exam(s) in order to qualify for a teaching internship certificate.

2. **Work through the Required Field Experience and Observation Hours learning module.** In this module, you will learn about the steps required for successfully completing the observation/field experience hours required as part of the teacher certification process. You must complete these hours in order to qualify for a teaching internship certificate.

   > **Note:** The required field observation hours were increased to 50 hours by the Texas Administration Agency for candidates beginning their program after Sept. 1, 2024. You must complete your background check prior to beginning your observations. Please visit the [TEA website](https://tea.texas.gov) for more information on completing the fingerprinting process.

3. **Get started on the course content in the Modules 1–6 (CIED 5301) learning module.** These content modules will equip you with the tools needed to be a successful Day 1 Ready Teacher. Completing Modules 1–6 satisfies the 150-seat hour requirement in order to qualify for a teaching internship certificate.

4. **Purchase or subscribe to the required e-text for this program.** Information on the e-text is located in the Modules 1–6 (CIED 5301) learning module.

## Next Steps

Once you have completed the first six modules, successfully passed your content area exam, and completed your required observation hours, you will be able to receive a **Statement of Eligibility** for your internship.

Upon completion of CIED 5301, you will be able to move on to the **Modules 7–14 (CIED 5302)** learning module and the **T-CAR Teacher Assessment Portfolio** learning module.
