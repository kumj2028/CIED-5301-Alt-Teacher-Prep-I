# Program Checklist

To help you stay on track, please follow this checklist to ensure you are completing all necessary components to successfully complete the program. Please note that the requirements prior to your internship can be completed in any order.

## Pre-Internship

- [ ] Complete the six modules in CIED 5301
- [ ] **Content Area Certification**
  - [ ] Take the practice test for your certification area and pass with 80%
  - [ ] Email the results of your practice test to [ACP@shsu.edu](mailto:ACP@shsu.edu) for approval to take the actual content test
  - [ ] Take and pass your content area certification exam
  - [ ] If you are seeking a core content EC-3, EC-6, core content 4-8, ELAR 4-8, or SS and ELAR 4-8 certification, you will also need to take and pass the **STR (Science of Teaching Reading)** exam
- [ ] Complete your required observation hours and record your observations in TK20
- [ ] Email the results of your content area exam to [ACP@shsu.edu](mailto:ACP@shsu.edu) once you have completed all of the above requirements
- [ ] Apply for positions in your certification area for your internship

## Internship

- [ ] Begin your internship as a teacher of record (minimum 180 days)
- [ ] Complete the six modules in CIED 5302 (can be completed prior to or during your internship)
- [ ] Record your internship through TK20
- [ ] Successfully pass your **PPR (Pedagogy and Professional Responsibilities)** exam
- [ ] Upon completion of all above requirements, apply for your standard teaching certification

---

For more information, please reach out to your professor or the program director at [ACP@shsu.edu](mailto:ACP@shsu.edu).
